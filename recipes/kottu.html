Kottu Roti

Description

Kottu Roti is a popular Sri Lankan street food made with chopped roti, vegetables, and meat or eggs. It's a flavorful and filling dish that is often served with spicy curry.

Ingredients

  • 4 roti (flatbreads)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken or beef (optional)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Chop the roti into small pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. If using, add cooked chicken or beef and stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  5. Push the mixture to one side of the pan and crack the eggs on the other side. Scramble the eggs until cooked.
  6. Add the chopped roti, soy sauce, curry powder, salt, and pepper. Mix everything together and cook for another 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
  8. Serve hot with spicy curry on the side.

Notes

Kottu Roti is highly customizable. You can add any vegetables or proteins you like. Adjust the spice level according to your preference. It's best enjoyed fresh and hot, but leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator for a day or two.
