Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  7. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  8. Add half of the ground beef and half of the marinara sauce.
  9. Sprinkle with half of the mozzarella cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers, ending with noodles, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese on top.
  11. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  14. Let it cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
  15. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
