Mac N Cheese

Description

Mac N Cheese is a classic comfort food made with macaroni pasta and a creamy cheese sauce. It's a simple yet delicious dish that can be enjoyed as a main course or a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  4. Add the flour, salt, pepper, and paprika. Stir for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Gradually whisk in the milk and cook until thickened.
  6. Stir in the cheddar and Parmesan cheese until melted.
  7. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and mix well.
  8. Transfer to a baking dish and sprinkle with breadcrumbs if desired.
  9. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until bubbly and golden on top.
  10. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.

Notes

You can customize this recipe by adding cooked bacon, vegetables, or different types of cheese. For a creamier texture, you can add more milk or cheese. This dish is best served hot and can be stored in the refrigerator for a few days.
